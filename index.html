What is Svadheeta?

A socially committed educational and charitable trust which aims to work for the quality enhancement of education. We are committed to provide academic and non academic support for students,teachers and institutions and conduct research studies for achieving our vision.We are committed to provide support and resources for the needy.

About Us

Svadheeta support for the quality of education

Svadheeta is a social organisation striving for the quality of the educational system. Our current educational system gives more importance for curriculum transactions than holistic education. To become a successful democratic country we should aim for higher quality of education for children from all sections of society.

Quality of education is a prerequisite for national, regional and global development. For the delivery of quality education, we need high quality teachers who are committed to teaching and equipped with necessary knowledge skills and competencies for effective teaching, institutions with adequate infrastructural and instructional facilities.

Vision

Our vision is to improve the quality of education in India, enable the educational institutions, empower teachers and students, provide support to socially marginalised sections and initiate and support research and socio-economic development activities


Mission

Savdheeta aims to address the disparities in the Indian education system by empowering every student, teachers and institutions to have higher quality education and provide assistance to socio economic development of country by following means.

  • Empower students by providing all necessary support to have higher quality of education.
  • Empower teachers by assisting them with all needed support to improving teaching quality and efficacy.
  • Support institutions for improving their overall quality.
  • Conduct any kind of helpful research to improve the quality of education system.
  • Provide assistance to government agencies in all quality improvement initiatives.
  • To engage in all kinds social, economic and educational development activities.
